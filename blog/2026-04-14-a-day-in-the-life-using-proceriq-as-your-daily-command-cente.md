By afternoon, you're facing actual decisions. Which feature should the team prioritize? How should you respond to that client concern? Is now the right time to restructure your process?

A productivity system without integration forces you to hunt for this context. You search for an old Slack thread. You skim three documents. You try to remember what you decided last quarter. The friction is real, and it costs energy.

Your daily workflow accelerates when context is automatic.
